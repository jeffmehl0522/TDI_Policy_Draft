CRITERIA FOR A CANDIDATE TO ATTEND A TRES DIAS WEEKEND

Several communities have requested direction from the Policy Committee concerning what qualifications, if any, should be applied to those who wish to attend a Tres Dias weekend as a Candidate/Pilgrim.

BACKGROUND: The following excerpts from the Essentials provide the necessary guidance:

3.1.1: That the candidate be sponsored for participation in all phases of the TRES DIAS Movement, rather than just the Weekend.

3.1.2: That candidates be sponsored by a member of a chartered TRES DIAS community or a similar community approved by TRES DIAS.

3.1.3: That candidates have a desire for a closer relationship with Jesus Christ as their Lord and Savior.

3.1.4: That candidates be accepted from all Christian denominations.

3.1.5: That candidates be at least 21 years of age. However, if a local secretariat, using its best judgment, finds a sound and compelling reason (such as spouse of a pescadore, or military personnel), it OCCASIONALLY, on a case by case basis, may accept a candidate who is at least 18 years of age.

3.1.6: That candidates have not previously made a TRES DIAS weekend or a similar experience recognized as equivalent by TRES DIAS.

3.2.1.F: It is a renewal experience for Christians and is not necessarily a conversion experience

LEADERSHIP: Anyone who wishes to be in a LEADERSHIP position within the Tres Dias ministry MUST be in compliance and agreement with the Qualifications for Community Leadership and accept the Tres Dias Statement of Belief without qualifications. These documents are included in the By-Laws of every Tres Dias community.

Furthermore, within Tres Dias a LEADER is defined as anyone who provides Servant Leadership. As such, these positions would include elected/voting/committee positions on the local Secretariat as well as any position of team service, including Auxiliaries/Chas/helpers, etc.

TRES DIAS STATEMENT OF BELIEF: The Committee also strongly recommends that the Tres Dias Statement of Belief be clearly displayed on the candidate application form. Although full agreement with the Statement of Belief by a Candidate/Pilgrim is not required, this will provide the necessary transparency for the person to understand the beliefs of the ministry he or she is about to participate in.

SUMMARY: ANYONE of age is welcome to attend a weekend, regardless of the state of their spiritual health as long as they are sponsored and are seeking a closer relationship with Jesus Christ as their Lord and Savior as outlined in the Essentials. And once they become a Pescadore there is nothing to prevent them from attending all Tres Dias events and activities. The ONLY limitations are related to leadership roles within Tres Dias as discussed above.

It must be stressed that Tres Dias is NOT designed to be an evangelistic effort but a renewal experience for Christians. To that end it is recommended that communities add the following statement to their candidate application forms whereby the candidate either checks a box or signs a statement to the effect:

I affirm that I have an open attitude and am seeking a closer relationship with Jesus Christ as my Lord and Savior.

-          NOTE: The failure of an applicant to agree to the above does not automatically preclude the person from attending a weekend, but it does require further conversation with the sponsor or someone who can bring further understanding.

Other areas of consideration, as always, would include the physical, mental and emotional status of the potential candidate/pilgrim and their ability to handle the stresses of a Tres Dias weekend, but thats a decision left to the sponsor and the local community.

Respectfully submitted,

Jeff Mehl

Jeff Mehl
Policy Committee Chair

REVISION NOTE: Verbiage was added to affirm that Tres Dias is not an evangelistic effort but a renewal experience for Christians; indicating that non-Christians should not be sponsored for weekends.
